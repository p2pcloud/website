Open marketplace

The Big Tech trio (Amazon, Google, and Microsoft) controls 65% of the market and dictates the prices. P2P Cloud is an open marketplace platform with direct competition. Full encryption and blockchain make open competition possible.

Every booking, every complaint on hosting provider is on the blockchain, accessible on the ledger.

Following blockchain values

At P2P Cloud, we follow the values of the blockchain – security, censorship resistance, and anonymity. Here's what you can expect:

Secure

No one but you has access to your VM or your data.

We will never request or change your keys – what you do with your data is your business.

Censorship resistant

Not controlled by a monolithic corporation. Because the data on each server is encrypted by nature, the server provider cannot stop or read any data stored on the hardware and stop service because of how the hardware is being utilized.

Anonymous

P2P Cloud is just a VM broker that automates every interaction via a smart contract to facilitate transactions and initiate the attestation process. The hosting provider has only your blockchain address, so you can stay anonymous.
