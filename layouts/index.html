How it works

  1. Book

    Book a VM on the blockchain. Choose by price, reliability, resources, and location

  2. Encrypt

    Encrypt a drive with LUKS and send it via IPFS. Bring your own key (BYOK).

  3. Confirm

    During attestation, the hosting provider cryptographically proves that you are the only person with access to the VM

  4. Launch

    Deliver your VPN keys and other secret info for launch through the attested secure channel

  5. Use

    Use it as regular VM. Treat p2pcloud's virtual machine as a regular VM from AWS or DigitalOcean. The performance penalty for end-to-end encryption is less than 10%.

Following blockchain values

Secure

No one but you has access to your VM or your data. We will never request nor change your keys as we cannot see them, ever. What you do with your data is your business, not ours, not the government's, not Big Tech's.

Censorship resistant

Not controlled by a monolithic corporation. Decentralized ownership. You alone have access to your keys.

Your data is hidden from everyone but you. The infrastructure is spread around the globe. Don't be like Parlor.

Anonymous

Hosting provider has only your blockchain address. Keep your name and billing address to yourself. We can't interact with your machines at all.

P2PCloud is just a VM broker; It sees nothing and knows nothing about you or your VMs.
