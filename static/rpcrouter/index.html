Web3 Geo-Load Balancer

A tool that distributes blockchain traffic among nodes in different regions to enhance response speed and reliability

Functionality

Balances traffic among blockchain nodes to optimize response times and reliability. Supported HTTP and WebSocket.

Regional Coverage

Available currently in North America, the European Union, and APAC, with expansion plans to include the Middle East and Latin America.

Balancing Strategies
Availability-Focused: Ensures continuous operation by prioritizing uptime and failover capabilities.
Latency-Focused: Reduces response time by selecting nodes with the lowest latency
